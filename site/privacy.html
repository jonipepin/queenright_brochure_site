Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Queenright ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information: Email address and profile details when you sign in (via Google, Apple, or email).
  • Apiary and hive data: Names, locations, inspection records, harvest data, queen status, and other beekeeping information you enter.
  • Photos: Images you add to inspections or hive records.

Automatically Collected Information

When you use the app, we may collect:

  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Location data: Used only when you set or edit apiary locations on the map. We do not continuously track your location.
  • Usage data: Analytics to improve the app (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Microsoft Clarity).

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Queenright app.
  • Store and sync your apiary, hive, and inspection data.
  • Authenticate your account and secure your data.
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and errors (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Understand how the app is used to improve the experience.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Crashlytics, Analytics, Remote Config. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Analytics and session insights. See Microsoft Privacy Statement.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud). We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials secure.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Access, update, or delete your account and data through the app or by contacting us.
  • Export your data (e.g., CSV export of inspections and harvests).

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app or at the contact information provided in the app store listing.
